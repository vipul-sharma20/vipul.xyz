Andrej Karpathy recently posted about [pattern for building personal knowledge
bases using LLMs][llm-wiki].

The main focus point is [something else][core-idea] but he mentioned about
[QMD][qmd] as a search engine on top of the wikis, docs etc.

> An on-device search engine for everything you need to remember. Index your
> markdown notes, meeting transcripts, documentation, and knowledge bases.
> Search with keywords or natural language. Ideal for your agentic flows.
> 
> QMD combines BM25 full-text search, vector semantic search, and LLM
> re-ranking—all running locally via node-llama-cpp with GGUF models.
